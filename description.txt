The Money Trough and Void Bag are pretty nice, so how about items that let you access your Safe and Defenders Forge from anywhere?

This mod introduces just that: a portable storage item to access you Safe or Defender's Forge from anywhere! They can both be obtained by shimmering either a Safe or Defender's Forge.
